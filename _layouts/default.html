I'm a 4th year Ph.D. student in the RISELab at UC Berkeley working with Prof. Ion Stoica and Prof. Joseph Gonzalez.

I'm currently interested in distributed systems for applications that must continuously interact with the real-world (e.g., robots and autonomous vehicles), and help develop:

  • ERDOS, a platform for the execution of robotics and autonomous vehicle applications.
  • Pylot, a modular autonomous vehicle pipeline that is able to drive vehicles in both real-world and simulation.
